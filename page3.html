Recommended Materials

Brush Pens

Here is a quick list of some of the best (and most popular) brush pens for lettering beginners –

1) Tombow dual brush pen
2) Tombow Fudenosuke
3) Crayola markers
4) Pentel arts pocket brush
5) Ecoline brush pens
6) Pentel Fude touch pen
7) Sharpie brush pens
8) Pentel Aquash water brush

Paper

Here are a few brands that you can’t go wrong with –

1) Rhodia – very popular among lettering artists, great quality paper.
2) Canson marker pad – insanely smooth paper to work with
3) HP Laser 32 – Great price-quality and quantity ratio. The best option for printing practice sheets!

Proper paper will keep your brushes from fraying and they will last longer (since the ink won’t get absorbed)
