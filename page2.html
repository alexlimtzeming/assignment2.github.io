An Introduction to Brush Calligraphy

Brush calligraphy is similar to the traditional calligraphy with the core difference of the tool being used for writing.

Brush calligraphy is performed using brush pens as well as regular brushes (for watercolors) whereas traditional calligraphy is performed using a dip pen and ink.
